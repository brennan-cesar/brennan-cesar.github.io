UUV Signal Conditioner Board

Designed and assembled a custom lever shifter PCB for UUV propeller control. Completed schematic and layout design, using shielding and low noise techniques throughout the board to retain signal integrity. Extensively tested board in test bench setup with an oscilloscope to measure noise, obtaining excellent signal integrity and maximum output range. Successfully implemented in full system.
